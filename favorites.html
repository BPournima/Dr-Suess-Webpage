Its a warm warm Christmas story. With a little tear of happiness trickling down my eye, the Grinch has become one of my favorite characters. A fun&thrilling reminder that everyone has goodness within to deserve a chance of forgiveness.

The most honest short story I've ever read. Those words put together in a way that they'll find their place in your heart. A book that tells you both good and bad happen - to always find our way through it. But no matter what, today is your day - Go move mountains.

The crux of this book is that anyone could do anything if they'd put their mind to it. A book with a brilliant story and moral.

An optimistic approach - to be grateful for the good things, strong for the hard things and brave for the bad things.

God!!This book was out of the world. An uninvited cat who turns the house upside down while he's having all the fun with the kids.I cant imagine a parent walking into all that mess. An entertaining book with great animated illustrations and a brilliant ending. I dont think my mother would believe the Cat in the hat story.*winks*
